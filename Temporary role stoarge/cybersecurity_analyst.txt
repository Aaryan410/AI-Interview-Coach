Cybersecurity Analyst


-- Scenario Based Questions --
Networking

Scenario 1

A company's internal website suddenly becomes inaccessible for employees in one office, while employees in other offices can still access it. Walk me through how you would troubleshoot the problem.

Scenario 2

You notice a sudden spike in outbound network traffic from a server at 3 AM. What steps would you take before deciding whether it is a security incident?

Scenario 3

A firewall rule was changed yesterday, and today several services have stopped working. How would you determine whether the firewall is responsible?

Scenario 4

Your IDS reports repeated port scans from an external IP address. What would you investigate, and what actions would you take?

Scenario 5

Several employees report intermittent VPN connectivity issues after a recent network update. How would you approach troubleshooting the problem?



Cryptography

Scenario 1

Your company wants to encrypt sensitive customer data stored in its database. How would you decide what should be encrypted, how keys should be managed, and what risks need to be considered?

Scenario 2

A developer suggests storing user passwords using Base64 encoding because it is "not human readable." How would you respond?

Scenario 3

Your SSL certificate expires tomorrow, but the replacement certificate has not yet been installed. What risks does this create, and how would you handle the situation?

Scenario 4

A company plans to rotate encryption keys across hundreds of servers. What challenges might arise during the process?

Scenario 5

After deploying HTTPS, users still receive browser security warnings. How would you investigate the cause?



Web Security

Scenario 1

A penetration test reveals an SQL Injection vulnerability in your login page. Describe your immediate response and your long-term solution.

Scenario 2

Users report that malicious JavaScript executes after viewing certain comments on your website. How would you investigate and mitigate the issue?

Scenario 3

Your application allows file uploads. What security risks would you consider before deploying this feature?

Scenario 4

A developer accidentally commits an API key to a public GitHub repository. What actions should be taken immediately?

Scenario 5

You discover that sensitive customer information appears in server logs. What would you do next?



Incident Response

Scenario 1

An employee clicks a phishing email and enters their company credentials. Walk me through your incident response process.

Scenario 2

Several workstations suddenly display ransomware messages. What are your first five actions?

Scenario 3

Your SIEM detects multiple failed login attempts followed by a successful login from an unusual location. How would you investigate?

Scenario 4

A critical production server appears to have been compromised. How would you preserve evidence while minimizing business disruption?

Scenario 5

You suspect an insider has copied confidential company documents. What evidence would you collect before drawing conclusions?



Penetration Testing

Scenario 1

During an internal penetration test, you discover a critical vulnerability. How would you document and communicate the finding to the client?

Scenario 2

A vulnerability scanner reports hundreds of issues. How would you prioritize which ones should be fixed first?

Scenario 3

A client asks you to perform a penetration test but provides almost no documentation about their environment. How would you begin?

Scenario 4

While performing a penetration test, you accidentally impact a production service. How should you respond?

Scenario 5

A client disputes one of your reported vulnerabilities, claiming it cannot be exploited. How would you validate your findings?



Identity & Access Management

Scenario 1

A former employee's account is still active two weeks after leaving the company. What risks does this create, and what process would you recommend?

Scenario 2

An administrator account appears to have logged in from two countries within one hour. How would you investigate?

Scenario 3

Your organization plans to roll out Multi-Factor Authentication to all employees. What challenges might users face, and how would you address them?

Scenario 4

A department requests permanent administrator privileges for all team members to simplify their workflow. How would you respond?

Scenario 5

You discover several unused privileged accounts that haven't been accessed in months. What actions would you take?
